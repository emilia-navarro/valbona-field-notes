# Valbona Valley: Technical Field Notes & Research Hub

## Repository Scope

This repository provides source-attributed field documentation for the Valbona Valley, Theth–Valbonë corridor, and related access routes in the Albanian Alps. It is designed as a research-oriented hub for logistical notes, trail metrics, and cultural-landscape observations.

The repository should be treated as a living field notebook, not as an official transport, safety, or protected-area authority. Operational details such as ferry schedules, transfer availability, snow conditions, and trail accessibility must be rechecked before field use.

## Geographic and Administrative Context

The working region is in northern Albania, within the Albanian Alps / *Alpet e Shqipërisë*. Since 2022, the protected-area framework has been consolidated under the Albanian Alps National Park / Parku Kombëtar i Alpeve, which includes the former Valbona Valley and Theth national park ecosystems and Lumi i Gashit.

Do not describe the Albanian side as “Prokletije National Park”; that name refers to the protected area in Montenegro. For Albania, use “Albanian Alps National Park”, “Alps of Albania National Park”, or the Albanian form “Parku Kombëtar i Alpeve”, depending on the audience.

## Evidence Standard

Each file uses the following confidence labels:

- **High:** official source, institutional source, or directly traceable dataset.
- **Medium:** reputable field guide, GPX-derived metric, or triangulated operational source.
- **Low:** field observation, local report, or unquantified trend that requires further validation.

Avoid hard quantitative claims unless the source and method are stated. For example, do not publish “tourism construction increased the ecological footprint by 25% since 2020” unless supported by a dated building-footprint dataset, satellite-image analysis, land-use classification, or municipal records.
