## Purpose

## 1. Maritime Transit: Koman–Fierzë Ferry Corridor

### Route

- **Primary route:** Koman → Fierzë / Fierzë → Koman.
- **Region:** Komani Lake / Drin river reservoir system, northern Albania.
- **Approximate function:** main scenic and logistical access route between Shkodër/Koman and the Valbona-side road network.

## 2. Terrestrial Transit: Fierzë / Bajram Curri / Valbonë

### Access Pattern

A common access sequence for Valbona is:

```text
Shkodër or Tirana → Koman → ferry to Fierzë → Bajram Curri / Valbonë transfer
```

Minibuses, shared vans, private transfers, and guesthouse-arranged pickups often coordinate around ferry arrivals, but this is operationally local and seasonal.

### Bajram Curri Node

- **Role:** regional road and service hub for the Valbona Valley approach.
- **Use in field planning:** transfer coordination, supplies, onward road transport.
- **Approximate onward transfer:** Fierzë/Bajram Curri to Valbonë varies by destination in the valley, road condition, and vehicle arrangement. Avoid publishing a single universal duration without date-stamped field confirmation.

### Recommended Field Protocol

Before departure:

1. Confirm ferry schedule and ticket conditions with the operator.
2. Confirm whether vehicle space is available, if traveling with a car.
3. Ask the destination guesthouse to confirm the current transfer pattern from Fierzë or Bajram Curri.
4. Recheck weather and road conditions during shoulder seasons.

- **Source:** ferry operator timetable; local-transfer pattern requires field confirmation.
- **Last verified:** 2026-07-01.
- **Method:** public-source review plus conservative operational wording.
- **Confidence:** High for ferry timetable; Medium/Low for road-transfer timing without same-week confirmation.
